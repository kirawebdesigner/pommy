A Burger Lover's Guide to Pommy Burger and Pizza

July 11, 2026 · Pommy Burger and Pizza

Start with the style you enjoy

Pommy's burger menu includes beef, cheese, double, fasting and chicken options. If you prefer a familiar combination, the Beef Burger brings together meat, onion, tomato, salad and mayonnaise. The Cheese Burger adds cheese, while the Double Burger is a larger choice.

Try a Pommy special

The Pommy Special Burger combines meat, egg, mayonnaise, chicken and beef slice. The Pommy Chicken Special Burger uses double chicken with egg, cheese, salad, onion and tomato. Check the full menu for current ETB prices before ordering.

Plan your order

Browse the menu, open any product to review its description, then add your choices to the cart. Pommy offers dine-in and takeaway in Addis Ababa, and the website can prepare a delivery or pickup order summary for confirmation.
