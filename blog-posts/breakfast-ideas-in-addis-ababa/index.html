Eggs and sandwiches

Egg Sandwich, Scrambled Egg, Omelette and Toast Bread with Egg are direct breakfast choices. Vegetable, tuna and club sandwiches offer more variety when you want something substantial.

Pommy's supplied menu also includes Pan Cake, Chechebsa with Black, Chechebsa with White, Special Fetira and Normal Fetira. Prices are listed in ETB on the full menu.
